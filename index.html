About Establishment
TEJ Catering Services was pionered in 2006 by Mrs Tejumola Yinka. Tejumola Yinka is a Nurse, Caterer, Wife & Mother. She established Royal Tej Catering Service in 2006. Royal Tej Catering Services caters for various events which includes wedding ceremonies, birthday parties, homecoming, graduation ceremonies e.t.c.
